About Us

The AWS Cloud Club is a student-led organization at Amity Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, dedicated to exploring the vast opportunities in cloud computing. Our mission is to foster a community of learners and innovators who are passionate about leveraging cloud technologies to solve real-world problems. Through workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects, we aim to equip our members with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Benefits of Joining

Networking Opportunities

Connect with peers, alumni, and industry professionals to build valuable relationships and explore career paths in cloud computing.

Hands-on Workshops

Participate in practical, project-based workshops to gain real-world experience with essential AWS services and tools.

Certification Support

Receive guidance, study resources, and peer support to prepare for and achieve valuable AWS certifications.
